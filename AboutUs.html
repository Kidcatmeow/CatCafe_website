About The Mewmew Café


Operating since January 2015, Mewmew Café is the fourth oldest continuously operating kitty café in Thailand. Our menu features award-winning espresso and coffee from Café Virtuoso and West Coast Coffee Roasters, pastries from Bread & Cie, cookies from The Cravory, and sandwiches from Sunshine & Orange.

The cats are available for adoption, should you fall in love during your visit. These furry friends are from The Rescue House, a non-profit organization dedicated to helping homeless kitties find loving homes.
